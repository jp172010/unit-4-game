Crystal Collector!

Game Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystalls below with hidden values. Each game their values will be randomly gernerated. By clicking on a crystal you will add its value to your score.

You win the game by matching your total score with the target number. If your total score goes above the target number you lose.
